My Player

This is a player for a game of poker squares. Poker squares is played by placing 25 cards as they are drawn into a 5x5 grid trying to make the best poker hand out of each column and row. Each type of hand has an associated score and all of the rows and columns scores are summed at the end of the game.

It plays the game based on a heuristic function that determines each column and row's potential in an attempt to find the best place to put the drawn card.
